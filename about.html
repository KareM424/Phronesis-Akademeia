About Me


Naina Budhwar

With a deep-rooted passion for language and communication, I’ve dedicated my career to helping students discover the power of English to express, create, and connect. I believe that strong language skills are not just academic tools—they're life skills that open doors to confidence, critical thinking, and meaningful self-expression. Drawing on 15 years of experience in teaching and mentoring students, I create a dynamic, engaging classroom environment where every child is encouraged to find their voice. Whether it's through storytelling, discussion, or writing, my goal is to nurture curiosity, build fluency, and inspire a lifelong love of language.




About the Classes


Empowering Young Minds Through Language Since 2011 Since 2017, our English classes have focused on building strong foundations in communication through speaking, vocabulary enhancement, grammar, and writing skills. With a passion for language and a commitment to student growth, we create an environment where children feel confident to express themselves, think critically, and develop a lasting command of English. Each class is designed to nurture fluency, clarity, and creativity—equipping students not just for academic success, but for real-world communication. Whether your child needs help with articulation, wants to strengthen their writing, or aims to enrich their vocabulary, we are here to guide them every step of the way.
